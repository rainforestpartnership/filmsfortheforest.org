2013 Celebrity Judges

  • Philippe Cousteau Jr.

    Philippe Cousteau Jr. has written, directed, and produced many environmental public service announcements. He is a correspondent for CNN, Animal Planet, and Planet Green.

  • Richard Linklater

    Linklater is the founder of the Austin Film Society but is better known for his films, including 'Fast Food Nation', 'Dazed and Confused', and 'Before Sunset'.

  • Dana Wheeler-Nicholson

    Dana Wheeler-Nicholson has appeared in numerous movies and TV shows, including 'Fletch', 'Tombstone', 'Fast Food Nation', 'Friday Night Lights', and 'Winter in the Blood'.

  • Jay Duplass

    Jay Duplass is an award-winning writer and director of several short films. His latest studio features include 'Cyrus', and 'Jeff Who Lives at Home'.
