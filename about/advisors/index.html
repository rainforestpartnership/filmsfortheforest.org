Advisors

Tommy Armour

Tommy is currently the music director for Earth Day Dallas, a two day environmental festival held at Fair Park in Dallas, TX. The inaugural event in 2011 featured over 250 exhibitors and 48,000 attendees making it the second largest Earth Day festival in the country. He spent the early part of his career as a manager for his father, PGA Tour professional golfer Tommy Armour III.

Elizabeth Avellán

Elizabeth Avellán, VP of Troublemaker Studios, is known as producer of the "Spy Kids" series, "Sin City", "Grindhouse", "Predator", "Machete", and other major features.

Jesse Lyda

Jesse is an independent filmmaker based in New York City and Austin, Texas. His first film was THE LEAST OF THESE which premiered at SXSW 2009 and later screened in both the Texas and U.S. Capitol buildings and multiple public interest festivals and screenings. THE LEAST OF THESE told the story of the U.S. Government's controversial policy of "family detention" of undocumented immigrants. Jesse's second film was a complete departure from the first - SATURDAY MORNING MASSACRE was a low-budget horror feature that premiered at LAFF 2012. Most recently Jesse served as executive producer of AN UNREAL DREAM: THE MICHAEL MORTON STORY (SXSW Audience Award Winner 2013) which is being broadcast on CNN and Discovery ID in 2013-14.
